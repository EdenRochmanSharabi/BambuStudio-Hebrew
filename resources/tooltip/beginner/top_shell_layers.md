#### Top Shell Layers (Top Solid Layers)

**What it does:** Sets how many fully solid layers are printed on the very top of your model to close it off.

**Why it matters:** Too few top layers can result in visible holes or pillowing (bumpy surfaces) because the solid layers sag into the infill gaps below them.

**Typical values:** 3-5 layers. Use more if you have low infill density, fewer if your infill is dense.

**Tip:** If you see small gaps or bumps on the top of your print, try adding 1-2 extra top layers before changing anything else.
