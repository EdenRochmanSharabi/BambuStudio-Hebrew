#### Print Speed

**What it does:** Controls how fast the printhead moves while laying down filament, measured in millimeters per second (mm/s).

**Why it matters:** Faster speeds reduce print time but can lower surface quality and cause issues like ringing (wavy patterns) or poor layer adhesion. Slower speeds produce cleaner, stronger results.

**Typical values:** 50-80 mm/s for general printing, 30-40 mm/s for high quality, 100+ mm/s for fast drafts (on capable printers).

**Tip:** Use the speed presets in BambuStudio rather than manually adjusting speeds -- they are tuned for your specific Bambu Lab printer.
