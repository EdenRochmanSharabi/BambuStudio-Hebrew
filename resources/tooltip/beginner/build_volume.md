#### Build Volume

**What it does:** Defines the maximum size (width, depth, and height) of objects your printer can produce in a single print.

**Why it matters:** If your model is larger than the build volume, it will not fit on the plate and you will need to split it into smaller parts and assemble them after printing.

**Typical values:** Bambu Lab A1 mini: 180 x 180 x 180 mm, A1: 256 x 256 x 256 mm, X1/P1 series: 256 x 256 x 256 mm.

**Tip:** If your model does not fit, use the scale tool in BambuStudio to shrink it, or use the cut tool to split it into pieces that can be glued together later.
