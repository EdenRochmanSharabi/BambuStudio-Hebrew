#### Brim

**What it does:** Prints a thin, flat ring of material around the base of your model on the first layer, increasing the contact area with the build plate.

**Why it matters:** A brim helps prevent warping and keeps prints with a small footprint firmly stuck to the bed during printing, especially for tall or narrow models.

**Typical values:** 3-8 mm brim width. Wider brims provide more adhesion but take longer to trim off afterward.

**Tip:** Use a brim when printing tall, thin objects or materials prone to warping. It peels off easily after the print cools.
