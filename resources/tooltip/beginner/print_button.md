#### Print Button

**What it does:** Sends the sliced G-code file to your printer to begin printing, either over Wi-Fi, LAN, or by exporting to an SD card.

**Why it matters:** This is the final step that starts your physical print. Once pressed, the printer will heat up and begin building your model layer by layer.

**Typical values:** Not applicable -- click after you have sliced and reviewed your model.

**Tip:** Make sure your build plate is clean and the correct filament is loaded before pressing Print. A quick check now avoids a failed print later.
