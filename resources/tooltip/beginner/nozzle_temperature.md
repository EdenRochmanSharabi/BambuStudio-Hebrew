#### Nozzle Temperature

**What it does:** Sets the temperature of the printer's nozzle (hotend), which melts the filament so it can be extruded.

**Why it matters:** Too low and the filament will not melt properly, causing clogs or weak layer bonding. Too high and the filament can burn, ooze excessively, or produce stringing between parts.

**Typical values:** PLA: 190-220°C, PETG: 230-250°C, ABS: 240-260°C, TPU: 220-240°C.

**Tip:** Use the temperature recommended on your filament spool's label as a starting point -- each brand can vary slightly even for the same material type.
