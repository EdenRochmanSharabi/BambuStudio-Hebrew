#### Bottom Shell Layers (Bottom Solid Layers)

**What it does:** Sets how many fully solid layers are printed on the very bottom of your model, forming its base.

**Why it matters:** Enough bottom layers create a strong, watertight floor for your print. Too few can result in a weak base that cracks or lets infill show through.

**Typical values:** 3-4 layers for most prints. Increase for heavy or load-bearing objects.

**Tip:** 3 bottom layers is usually enough -- you rarely need to change this unless you are printing something that takes a lot of stress on the bottom.
