#### Print Settings Tab

**What it does:** Contains all the settings that control how your model is physically printed, including layer height, wall count, infill, speed, and support options.

**Why it matters:** These settings have the biggest impact on print quality, strength, and time. Choosing the right profile here is the difference between a great print and a failed one.

**Typical values:** BambuStudio provides preset profiles (like 0.20mm Standard or 0.12mm Fine) that work well out of the box.

**Tip:** Start with a built-in preset and only tweak individual settings after you understand what each one does. Changing too many things at once makes it hard to diagnose problems.
