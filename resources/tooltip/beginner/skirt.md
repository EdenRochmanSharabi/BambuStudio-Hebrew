#### Skirt

**What it does:** Prints a few loops of filament around (but not touching) your model before the actual print begins.

**Why it matters:** The skirt primes the nozzle, ensuring a steady flow of filament before printing starts. It also lets you visually confirm the first layer is sticking properly before the model itself begins.

**Typical values:** 1-3 loops at 3-5 mm distance from the model.

**Tip:** Watch the skirt print -- if it looks patchy or does not stick, you can cancel and adjust your bed level before wasting time on the full model.
