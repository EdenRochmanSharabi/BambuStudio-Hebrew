#### Wall & Infill Print Order

**What it does:** Determines whether the printer draws the outer walls first or fills the inside (infill) first on each layer.

**Why it matters:** Printing walls before infill generally produces a cleaner, smoother outer surface because the walls are not pushed outward by infill. Printing infill first can give the walls more support from behind, improving strength.

**Typical values:** "Inner/Outer" or "Outer/Inner" for wall order; infill usually prints after walls by default.

**Tip:** Leave the default order unless you notice surface quality issues -- the preset chosen by BambuStudio already works well for most cases.
