#### Arrange

**What it does:** Automatically positions all models on the build plate to fit efficiently, spacing them out so they do not overlap.

**Why it matters:** Good arrangement makes the best use of your build plate area, lets you print more objects at once, and ensures models have enough clearance from each other and the edges.

**Typical values:** Not applicable -- click the Arrange button and the slicer handles placement for you.

**Tip:** After auto-arranging, double-check that no model is hanging off the edge of the plate. You can still drag individual models to fine-tune their positions.
