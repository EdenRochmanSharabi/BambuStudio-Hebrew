#### Infill Density

**What it does:** Controls what percentage of the inside of your model is filled with material -- the rest is empty space.

**Why it matters:** Higher infill makes the print stronger and heavier but uses more filament and takes longer. Lower infill saves time and material while still being surprisingly strong for most uses.

**Typical values:** 10-15% for decorative items, 15-20% for general use, 40-60% for functional parts, 100% for completely solid.

**Tip:** 15% infill is enough for most prints. You rarely need more than 20% unless the part will bear significant loads or stress.
