#### Filament Settings Tab

**What it does:** Contains settings specific to the filament material you are using, including nozzle and bed temperatures, cooling fan speeds, and retraction behavior.

**Why it matters:** Every filament material (and even different brands of the same material) prints best at slightly different temperatures and speeds. Correct filament settings prevent clogs, stringing, warping, and weak layers.

**Typical values:** Use the preset matching your filament brand and type (e.g., "Bambu PLA Basic" or "Generic PETG").

**Tip:** When using Bambu Lab filament, scan the spool's RFID tag and the printer will automatically load the correct filament profile for you.
