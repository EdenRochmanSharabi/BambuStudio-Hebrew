#### Preview Tab

**What it does:** Shows a detailed layer-by-layer visualization of your sliced model, displaying the exact toolpaths the printer will follow.

**Why it matters:** Previewing before printing lets you catch problems like missing supports, insufficient infill, or unexpected travel moves -- saving you filament, time, and frustration.

**Typical values:** Not applicable -- switch to this tab after slicing your model.

**Tip:** Use the layer slider on the right to scrub through layers one at a time. Pay special attention to the first layer (bed adhesion), overhangs, and the top surface.
