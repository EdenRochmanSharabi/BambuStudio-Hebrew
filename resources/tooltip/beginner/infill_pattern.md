#### Infill Pattern

**What it does:** Determines the geometric pattern used to fill the inside of your model (grid, triangles, honeycomb, gyroid, and more).

**Why it matters:** Different patterns offer different trade-offs between strength, print speed, and flexibility. Some patterns are stronger in certain directions while others distribute force more evenly.

**Typical values:** Grid (fast and simple), Gyroid (strong in all directions, flexible), Triangles (good all-around strength), Honeycomb (efficient strength-to-weight).

**Tip:** Gyroid is an excellent all-purpose choice for beginners -- it is strong in every direction and prints reliably.
