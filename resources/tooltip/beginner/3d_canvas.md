#### 3D Canvas

**What it does:** The main visual workspace in the center of BambuStudio where your 3D models are displayed on a virtual build plate.

**Why it matters:** The canvas is where you interact with your models -- selecting, moving, rotating, and scaling them. It gives you a real-time preview of how objects will be positioned on the physical build plate.

**Typical values:** Not applicable -- this is the central view that is always visible in the 3D Editor tab.

**Tip:** Use the scroll wheel to zoom, right-click and drag to rotate the view, and middle-click and drag to pan. These controls make it easy to inspect your model from every angle.
