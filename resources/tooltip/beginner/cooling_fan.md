#### Cooling Fan

**What it does:** Controls the part cooling fan that blows air onto filament right after it leaves the nozzle, solidifying it quickly.

**Why it matters:** Proper cooling is essential for clean overhangs, bridges, and small details. Without enough cooling, filament stays soft too long and droops or curls. Too much cooling on some materials can cause poor layer adhesion.

**Typical values:** PLA: 100% fan after the first few layers. PETG: 30-60%. ABS: usually off or very low.

**Tip:** For PLA, always run the fan at full speed after the first layer -- it dramatically improves overhang quality and fine detail.
