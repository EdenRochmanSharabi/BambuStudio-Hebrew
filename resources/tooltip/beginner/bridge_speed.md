#### Bridge Speed

**What it does:** Sets the speed at which the printer extrudes filament when bridging across a gap with no support underneath.

**Why it matters:** Bridging speed is a balancing act: too slow and the filament sags under its own weight, too fast and it may not stick to the anchor points on either side.

**Typical values:** 20-40 mm/s, significantly slower than normal print speed.

**Tip:** If your bridges are drooping, try increasing the fan speed to maximum during bridging before slowing down -- cooling the filament quickly is often more effective than speed alone.
