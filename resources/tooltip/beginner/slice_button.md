#### Slice Button

**What it does:** Converts your 3D model and all your chosen settings into G-code -- the step-by-step instructions that tell your printer exactly how to move, extrude, and heat.

**Why it matters:** Slicing is the essential step between having a 3D model on screen and having a file your printer can actually execute. Without slicing, your printer has no instructions to follow.

**Typical values:** Not applicable -- just click the button when your model and settings are ready.

**Tip:** After slicing, always switch to the Preview tab to inspect the result layer by layer. This can reveal issues like missing supports or unexpected infill before you waste filament.
