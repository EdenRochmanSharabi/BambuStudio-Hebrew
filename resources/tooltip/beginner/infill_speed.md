#### Infill Speed

**What it does:** Sets how fast the printer moves while filling the interior of your model with the infill pattern.

**Why it matters:** Since infill is hidden inside the print and does not need to look perfect, it can be printed faster than outer walls without affecting appearance. This helps reduce overall print time.

**Typical values:** 80-150 mm/s, often 1.5-2x faster than the outer wall speed.

**Tip:** You can safely increase infill speed above your wall speed since nobody sees the inside of the print -- it is a free way to save time.
