#### Wall Loops (Perimeters)

**What it does:** Sets how many outlines (shells) are drawn around the outside of each layer before the inside is filled.

**Why it matters:** More walls make your print stronger and give the outer surface a more solid appearance, but they add print time and use more filament.

**Typical values:** 2 walls for decorative items, 3 walls for functional parts that need strength, 4+ for maximum durability.

**Tip:** For most prints, 2-3 walls is a great starting point -- you get a strong shell without wasting time or material.
