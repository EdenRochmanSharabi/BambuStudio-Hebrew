#### Device Monitor

**What it does:** Lets you watch a live camera feed of your printer, monitor print progress, check temperatures, and control the printer remotely from within BambuStudio.

**Why it matters:** You can keep an eye on your print from another room or even another location, and quickly pause or stop the print if something goes wrong -- saving filament and preventing damage.

**Typical values:** Not applicable -- connect your printer to your network and it will appear in the Device Monitor panel.

**Tip:** Check in on your print during the first few layers via the camera. Most failures happen early, and catching them quickly saves hours of wasted printing.
