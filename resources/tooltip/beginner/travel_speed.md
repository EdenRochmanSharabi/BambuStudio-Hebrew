#### Travel Speed

**What it does:** Controls how fast the printhead moves when it is not extruding filament, such as when repositioning between different parts of the model.

**Why it matters:** Faster travel reduces oozing and stringing because the nozzle spends less time dripping over open areas. It also shortens overall print time.

**Typical values:** 150-250 mm/s. Travel speed is typically the fastest movement the printer makes.

**Tip:** Keep travel speed high -- fast moves between print areas mean less time for filament to ooze out of the nozzle, resulting in a cleaner print.
