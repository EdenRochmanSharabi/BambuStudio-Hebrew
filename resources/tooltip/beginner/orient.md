#### Orient (Auto-Orient)

**What it does:** Automatically rotates your model to find the best orientation for printing, minimizing the need for supports and improving surface quality.

**Why it matters:** The orientation of a model on the build plate dramatically affects how much support material is needed, how strong the print is, and how good the surface finish looks.

**Typical values:** Not applicable -- click the Orient button to let the slicer calculate the optimal rotation.

**Tip:** Auto-orient is a great starting point, but for mechanical parts you may want to manually orient so that the strongest layer direction aligns with the load the part will carry.
