#### Support Type

**What it does:** Chooses between normal (grid/line) supports and tree-shaped supports that branch up to reach overhangs.

**Why it matters:** Normal supports are simple and reliable but use more material and can be harder to remove. Tree supports use less material, are easier to remove, and often leave a cleaner surface on the model.

**Typical values:** Normal for flat overhangs and simple shapes; Tree for organic or complex models with many small overhangs.

**Tip:** Try tree supports first -- they are easier to snap off and usually leave fewer marks on your print.
