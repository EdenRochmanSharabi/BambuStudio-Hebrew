#### First Layer Height

**What it does:** Controls the thickness of the very first layer printed onto the build plate.

**Why it matters:** A thicker first layer squishes more filament against the bed, giving your print a stronger foundation and much better adhesion so it does not come loose mid-print.

**Typical values:** 0.20 mm to 0.28 mm. Often set slightly thicker than the rest of the layers.

**Tip:** If your prints keep detaching from the bed, try increasing the first layer height by 0.04 mm -- the extra squish can make a big difference.
