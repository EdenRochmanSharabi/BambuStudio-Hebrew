#### Layer Height

**What it does:** Sets the thickness of each individual layer the printer stacks to build your model.

**Why it matters:** Thinner layers produce smoother surfaces and finer detail, but take much longer to print; thicker layers print faster but look rougher.

**Typical values:** 0.20 mm for a good balance of speed and quality, 0.12 mm for fine detail, 0.28 mm for fast draft prints.

**Tip:** Start with 0.20 mm for your first prints -- it works well for most models and keeps print times reasonable.
