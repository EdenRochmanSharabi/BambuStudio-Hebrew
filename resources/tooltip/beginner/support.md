#### Support Structures

**What it does:** Adds temporary scaffolding beneath overhanging parts of your model so the printer has something to build on instead of printing into thin air.

**Why it matters:** Without supports, steep overhangs and floating sections will droop, curl, or fail completely because melted filament cannot hold itself up with nothing underneath.

**Typical values:** Enable supports when your model has overhangs steeper than about 45 degrees from vertical or has parts floating above the build plate.

**Tip:** Supports are removed after printing. They may leave small marks on the surface, so orient your model to minimize supports on visible faces when possible.
