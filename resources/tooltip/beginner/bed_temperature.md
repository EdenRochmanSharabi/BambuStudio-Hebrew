#### Bed Temperature

**What it does:** Sets the temperature of the heated build plate, which keeps the bottom of your print warm while it is being built.

**Why it matters:** A warm bed helps the first layer stick firmly and prevents warping (corners lifting off the plate), especially with materials that shrink as they cool.

**Typical values:** PLA: 55-65°C, PETG: 70-85°C, ABS: 90-110°C, TPU: 50-60°C.

**Tip:** If your first layer is not sticking, try increasing bed temperature by 5°C. If the bottom of the print looks rough or "elephant foots" outward, try lowering it by 5°C.
