#### Timelapse

**What it does:** Takes a photo at the end of each layer (or at set intervals) and combines them into a smooth time-lapse video of your print being built.

**Why it matters:** Timelapses are a fun way to share your prints and can also help you spot issues by reviewing how the print progressed layer by layer.

**Typical values:** Enabled or disabled. "Traditional" mode takes photos without moving the head; "Smooth" mode moves the head out of the way for a cleaner video.

**Tip:** Smooth timelapse mode looks great but adds a small amount of extra print time because the printhead must park for each photo.
