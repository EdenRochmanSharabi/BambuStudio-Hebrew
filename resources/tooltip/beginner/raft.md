#### Raft

**What it does:** Prints a thick, multi-layer platform underneath your entire model, which you peel off after printing.

**Why it matters:** A raft provides maximum bed adhesion and a perfectly level foundation, which is especially helpful when printing on uneven beds or with warping-prone materials like ABS.

**Typical values:** 2-3 raft layers with a small air gap between the raft and the model for easy removal.

**Tip:** Rafts use extra filament and time, so only use one when a brim is not enough. A raft is a last resort for adhesion problems, not an everyday setting.
